Introduction¶

The Prosper Loan Dataset is a collection of data from the prosper lending online business, The dataset provide insightful information on loan status,occupation, borrowerstate, loandate, loanrate, creditgrade, monthlyincome, investors, percentfunded etc. It also provide information on fullfilled and unfilled application, credit risk assessment, borrowers behaviour.

plt.figure(figsize=(30,20))
ax = sns.countplot(x='loanoriginationquarter', data=df, color=sns.color_palette()[8])

plt.title('loanoriginationquarter')
plt.xlabel('loanoriginationquarter')
plt.ylabel('Count')

total_count = len(df)  

for container in ax.containers:
    for bar in container:
        count = bar.get_height()
        percent = (count * 100) / total_count
        ax.annotate(f'{percent:.1f}%', xy=(bar.get_x() + bar.get_width() / 2, bar.get_height()),
                    ha='center', va='bottom', fontsize=12, color='black',fontweight='bold')

comment¶

The loanoriginationquarter in the analysis shows the different years and quarters of the origin of prosper loan and also shows the year and quarter that have the highest lending value. the origination of prosper loan quarter in the dataset was in 2005 Q4 and the lending value was (22), in 2013 Q4 they have the highest lending value (14450), followed by 2014 Q1 they have (12172) and so on in the dataset.
